Token Proposal

OSUI Range Slider wraps noUiSlider as .osui-range-slider (host) + .noUi-target (track). The handle sits on a .noUi-origin positioned via translate(), and the fill uses .noUi-connect with translate + scale from a top-left origin. Proposed uses a flat look: a light gray pill track, primary filled range, and a white circular thumb with a 2px ring in the same primary color (no drop shadow).
