Token proposal

os-ui-228-current-scoped.css wires tooltip to legacy --color-neutral-9 / --color-neutral-0. os-ui-229-new-tokens-scoped.css switches to --token-text-default + --token-text-inverse and explicit tooltip custom properties. Proposed: keep max-width on a --osui-tooltip-max-width token instead of 250px magic number.
